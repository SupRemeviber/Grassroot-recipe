Breif

Pizza is a delicious and appetizing meal that can be enjoyed with chilled soft drinks

ingredients

-1 cup all purpose flour -4 tablespoon processed cheese -50 mililitre tomato ketchup -2 mushroom(optional can use sausages or meat) -1 tomato -1/4 capsicum (green pepper) -1 onion -1/2 teaspoon oregano -1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes -1/4 cup mozzarella -1/2 teaspoon baking powder -1/2 tablespoon dry yeast -1/2 teaspoon sugar

Preparation

Take a dough kneading plate and add all-purpose flour to it. Add salt and baking powder in it and sieve the flour once. Now make a hole in the centre and add 1 teaspoon of oil to it.
